Confidence Booster


Duration: 6 hours
Students: min 8 max 40
Level: All levels

Beneficial to all levels of student and a good introduction to the methods of Acting Solution.

This workshop is aimed at students that have a good grasp of theoretical English but do not have the opportunity to practice in real life situations.

Students are led through a series of drama activities and simple workplace role plays that build confidence as a group.

Workshop facilitators establish a safe environment and relaxed atmosphere which allows students to experiment and practice with existing language skills and knowledge.
