Corporate Visualizations


Duration: Min 12 hours
Students: Flexible
Level: Intermediate and above

This package is for clients wishing to visualize any new concept,theme or implement your own training ideas with our methodology and expertise.

Example: A sales manager wants to initiate a new sales tactic. Acting Solution will role play with your team to explore the pros and cons of the tactics?

Example: A HR manager wants to train staff about conducting job interviews. Acting solution uses actors to be employees in mock interviews to help the team experience as many personality types as possible.

The versatility of our actor / teachers and workshop style mean that as long as you,the client,have a clear idea of what needs to be achieved we can use acting to help you find a path to the solution.
