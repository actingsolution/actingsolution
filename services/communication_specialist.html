Communication Specialist


Duration: 12 hours (2 days)
Students: min 8 max 30
Level: Elementary and higher

This workshop is aimed at students that need to speak on a daily basis with foreign bosses, clients or customers and need to communicate clearly and efficiently.

Each activity and scenario will limit the students from communicating in a certain way. The students are guided to find alternative methods of communication to make themselves understood.

Facilitators will help with communication tips and how to be understood no matter what the subject matter of level of English.
