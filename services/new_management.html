Management skills


Duration: Minimum 12 hrs
Students: Flexible
Level: Intermediate and above

The core principle behind role play training is that a group of actors will first perform a scene or series of scenes that reflect the subject matter you want to address. Acting Solution will devise a personalized role play situation which you first observe, identify the issues then interact to adapt to a better and improved outcome.

Aimed at new management or existing managers that want to improve areas or learn new styles of management. Each mini scene will be about a different aspect or trial new managers might face. Students will then be given the chance to interact with the actors in the same scenes but with different approaches to try and achieve a positive ending.
